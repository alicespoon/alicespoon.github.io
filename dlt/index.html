DLT is Saint Petersburg’s oldest department store that opened back in 1909. Today it is the flagship store of  TSUM Moscow — Russia’s main luxury department store.

The lookbook was created as a part of the annual spring fashion show. In 2019, the show was curated by Julia Pelipas (Vogue UA).
